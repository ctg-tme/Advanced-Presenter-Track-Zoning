Guide

Compatibility

Digital Presenter Track only. Use with Cisco P60 or Cisco PTZ4K.

Key

  • Triangle: start
  • Circle: middle points
  • Square: end

Workflow

  1. Upload a P60/PTZ4K camera screenshot.
  2. Left click points or use Add Shape.
  3. Use Snap and Grid when alignment matters.
  4. Drag points to adjust; use Undo and Redo for history.
  5. Copy and save under xConfiguration Cameras PresenterTrack TriggerZone

Notes

Do not repeat the first point. Avoid intersecting lines.

Help Guide

What This Tool Does

Build Cisco Digital Presenter Track TriggerZone coordinates for Cisco P60 and Cisco PTZ4K cameras. This workflow does not apply to Motorized Presenter Track.

Build A Zone

Upload a camera screenshot, click points on the editor, or use Add Shape to drag out a starter polygon. Drag any point to refine the final zone.

Placement Helpers

Snap to Grid is enabled by default and shows a placement preview. Adjust Grid spacing when you need coarser or finer alignment.

Coordinates

Type or paste x,y coordinate pairs. Complete pairs apply automatically; incomplete pairs are ignored until finished and are highlighted without changing your text.

Shortcuts

  • Cmd/Ctrl+C copies valid coordinates.
  • Cmd/Ctrl+V pastes coordinates into the editor.
  • Cmd/Ctrl+Z undoes the last zone action.
  • Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+Z or Cmd/Ctrl+Y redoes it.

Save

Copy and save under xConfiguration Cameras PresenterTrack TriggerZone . Valid coordinates are also written to the URL hash as dptCoordinates.
